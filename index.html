The most costly operational problems are rarely visible in dashboards. They live in the gap between how systems are designed and how they're actually used.

Most reporting systems don't fail — they rely on people to keep them running.

Stability is often maintained through manual intervention, not system design. When key people leave, the fragility is exposed.
